# Text masking

The following are secrete text strings that we want to copy to clipboard but don't want to
discover to those who observe our screen. This may be useful, for example, when we communicate
to a support service and share our screen.

> __NOTE!__ __1.__ This solution is not secure at all as the secrete data is kept unprotected.
> It just prevents this data from visual observation.
> __2.__ After using the copied secret text don't forget to remove this text from clipboard by
> copying some other text there.
